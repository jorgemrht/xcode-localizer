# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be open, respectful, and constructive for everyone who participates. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experiences and viewpoints.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Assume positive intent, and take responsibility when something needs correcting.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the quality of the skill.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive behavior.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be inappropriate in a professional open-source project.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and any public communication where someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not follow this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project.

Enforcement decisions should be fair, proportionate, and focused on protecting a respectful contributor environment.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/jorgemrht/xcode-localizer/issues) or contacting the maintainer directly through GitHub when a private channel is needed.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and with full respect for the privacy and safety of everyone involved. Reporters will not face retaliation.
