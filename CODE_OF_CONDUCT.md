# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the project.

- Treat everyone with respect — harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language will not be tolerated.
- Keep discussions on-topic and assume positive intent.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.

Violations may result in comments, issues, or contributions being removed. Repeated or serious violations may lead to exclusion from the project.

To report an issue, open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/jorgemrht/xcode-localizer/issues) or contact the maintainer directly on GitHub.
